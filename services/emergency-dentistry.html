Emergency Dentistry

Same-day care when
you need it most.

Severe pain, a broken tooth, a knocked-out tooth, or sudden swelling — we keep emergency slots open every weekday so you don't have to wait.

When dental pain can't
wait.

Dental emergencies don't follow a schedule. A cracked tooth, lost filling, abscess, or knocked-out tooth needs prompt, calm attention — and we've built our day around being able to fit you in.

Call us first thing in the morning if you're in pain. We'll do everything we can to see you the same day, often within hours, and start you on the right path to relief.

  • Same-day emergency appointments
  • Pain relief & abscess care
  • Cracked & broken tooth repair
  • Knocked-out tooth re-implantation
  • Lost crown or filling replacement
