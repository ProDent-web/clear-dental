Why professional
whitening matters.

Over-the-counter whitening strips can only do so much — and they can cause uneven results, gum irritation, and tooth sensitivity. Professional whitening uses a higher concentration of safe, peroxide-based gel under controlled conditions, giving you dramatic results without the downsides.

We offer two options — choose the one that fits your schedule and goals.

  • In-Office Whitening — Up to 8 shades brighter in a single one-hour visit.
  • Take-Home Custom Trays — Whitening you control, with custom-fit trays and professional gel.
  • Combination Plan — Start in-office, maintain at home for the longest-lasting results.
