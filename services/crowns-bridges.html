When you need
a crown.

A dental crown is a custom-made cap that fits over a damaged or weakened tooth — restoring its shape, strength, and appearance. Crowns are commonly used after root canals, large fillings, or to protect a cracked tooth from further damage.

We craft every crown using high-strength porcelain or zirconia, color-matched to blend seamlessly with your natural teeth.

  • Porcelain & zirconia crowns
  • Same-day crowns available for select cases
  • Implant crowns (paired with our implant service)

When you need
a bridge.

A bridge replaces one or more missing teeth by anchoring a replacement to the healthy teeth on either side. It restores your bite, prevents your other teeth from shifting, and brings back the appearance of a complete smile.

For patients who'd prefer a permanent option that doesn't rely on neighboring teeth, we also offer implant-supported bridges.

  • Traditional fixed bridges
  • Implant-supported bridges
  • Cantilever & Maryland bridges (case-by-case)
