What is cosmetic dentistry?

Cosmetic dentistry is the art of improving the appearance of your smile — color, shape, alignment, and proportion — while maintaining the strength and function of your teeth. At Clear Dental, every cosmetic case begins with a careful conversation about what you actually want, followed by a preview of what's possible.

We specialize in restorations that look like nature intended — translucent edges, natural shading, and proportions that complement your face.

  • Porcelain veneers (minimal-prep and traditional)
  • Composite bonding for chips and small gaps
  • Gum contouring for a balanced smile line
  • Full smile makeovers with digital previews
