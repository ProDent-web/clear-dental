Endodontics

Save the tooth.
Save the smile.

Modern root canal therapy is nothing like the procedure of old — it's quiet, comfortable, and the best way to keep a damaged tooth for the long haul.

Root canals,
reimagined.

Root canal therapy treats infection or damage deep inside a tooth — and saves the tooth from extraction. With modern instruments, microscopy, and gentle technique, most patients describe the procedure as no different than getting a filling.

When a tooth has decay or trauma that's reached the inner pulp, a root canal is almost always the better option than removing the tooth entirely. Your natural tooth is worth saving.

  • Endodontic therapy (root canals)
  • Retreatment of failed root canals
  • Post-treatment crown to protect the tooth
  • Sedation options for anxious patients
