When a tooth needs to
come out.

Sometimes the kindest thing for a tooth — and your overall oral health — is to remove it. Whether it's a damaged tooth beyond repair, an impacted wisdom tooth, or surgical prep for implants, we approach every procedure with the same priority: your comfort.

We offer multiple sedation options and walk you through everything before we begin. Most patients are surprised at how routine and gentle the experience feels.

  • Routine & surgical extractions
  • Wisdom teeth removal
  • Pre-implant site preparation
  • Bone grafting
  • Sedation options for anxious patients
