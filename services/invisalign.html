Invisalign®

Straighter teeth.
Without the metal.

Clear, custom aligners that gently move your teeth into place — comfortable, removable, and nearly invisible. Most cases finish in 6–18 months.

A modern alternative
to traditional braces.

Invisalign uses a series of clear, removable aligners — each one custom-made for your teeth — to gradually shift them into the desired position. You wear each aligner for about two weeks, then switch to the next.

Because they're removable, you can eat, drink, and brush exactly as you normally would. And because they're clear, most people won't even notice you're wearing them.
