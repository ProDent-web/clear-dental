Preventative Dentistry

Stop problems
before they start.

The most cost-effective dentistry you can invest in. Routine cleanings, thorough exams, and personalized guidance to keep your smile healthy for life.

Care that compounds
over time.

Most dental problems start small and silent. By the time they hurt, they're more complicated and more expensive to fix. Preventative care catches everything early — when treatment is simpler, gentler, and more affordable.

We recommend a cleaning and exam every six months for most patients. Some need to come more often; some can stretch it out. We'll build the right cadence around your needs.

  • Routine cleanings & periodic exams
  • Digital X-rays (low-radiation, on demand)
  • Oral cancer screening
  • Fluoride treatments & sealants
  • Personalized at-home care plan
