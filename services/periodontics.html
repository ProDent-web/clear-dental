Your gums are the foundation of your smile — and of your overall health. We treat gum disease at every stage with proven, gentle therapy.

More than just
a cleaning.

Periodontal (gum) disease is the leading cause of tooth loss in adults — and it's almost always preventable when caught early. Bleeding gums, persistent bad breath, or gum recession can all be signs that your gums need more than a routine cleaning.

We offer the full range of periodontal therapy, from non-surgical deep cleanings to ongoing maintenance care for patients with a history of gum disease.

  • Periodontal evaluation & charting
  • Scaling & root planing (deep cleaning)
  • Localized antibiotic therapy
  • Periodontal maintenance program
  • Gum disease prevention education
