Comprehensive
preventive care.

Routine preventive care is the foundation of a healthy smile — and the most cost-effective dentistry you can invest in. We recommend cleanings and exams every six months, with digital imaging as needed to catch problems early.

Our hygienists are gentle, thorough, and skilled at making even nervous patients feel at ease.

  • Cleanings & periodic exams
  • Digital X-rays (low-radiation)
  • Oral cancer screening
  • Fluoride treatments & sealants
  • Tooth-colored fillings
  • Periodontal (gum) therapy
  • Pediatric-friendly visits
  • Emergency dental care
