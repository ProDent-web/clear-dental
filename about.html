Background

From Virginia
to Michigan
and back.

Dr. Victor James Lim grew up in Northern Virginia. His thirst for knowledge took him to sunny California, where he majored in Biology at California Polytechnic Institute. He then went to the snowy cold of Michigan to attend the University of Michigan, School of Dentistry, for his Doctorate in Dental Surgery.

Since then, Dr. Lim has practiced in a range of dental environments — including high-end cosmetic and multi-doctor practices — continually investing his time in attaining the latest dental techniques through continuing education courses. He dedicates himself to providing comprehensive, quality dentistry.

  • DDS — University of Michigan, School of Dentistry
  • BS, Biology — California Polytechnic Institute
  • Focus — Cosmetic & comprehensive dentistry
  • Continuing Education — Ongoing advanced training
Our Philosophy

Four ideas
guide everything.

No two patients are the same — and no two treatment plans should be either. These are the principles we return to, every single visit.

01

Listen first

Before we recommend anything, we want to understand your concerns, your goals, and your history with dental care.

02

Plan thoughtfully

Every treatment is sequenced for the best long-term outcome — never rushed, never over-engineered.

03

Execute with precision

Digital scanners, magnification, and proven materials — the details that make a restoration last.

04

Care for the whole person

Your comfort, your time, and your trust are as important as the work itself.
